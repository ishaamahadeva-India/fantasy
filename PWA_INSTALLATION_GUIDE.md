Your app is configured as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** and can be installed on devices!

### How Users Can Install the App:

#### **On Desktop (Chrome, Edge, Safari):**
1. Visit `https://www.quizzbuzz.in`
2. Look for the **install icon** in the browser address bar (usually a "+" or download icon)
3. Click "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"
4. The app will be installed and can be launched like a native app

#### **On Android:**
1. Visit `https://www.quizzbuzz.in` in Chrome
2. A banner will appear at the bottom: **"Add quizzbuzz to Home screen"**
3. Tap "Add" or "Install"
4. The app icon will appear on the home screen
5. Tap the icon to launch the app in standalone mode

#### **On iOS (iPhone/iPad):**
1. Visit `https://www.quizzbuzz.in` in Safari
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Customize the name if needed
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. The app icon will appear on the home screen
